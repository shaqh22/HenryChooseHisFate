At first Henry dosent notice any changes in his new life.

He has not gained or lost anything in the material form.

So he assumes that it must all have been a dream in his head.

And so he goes on to live his life as he normally would before all of these strange things happened.

Until one day on his eighteenth birthday when he becomes a man.

He meets a beautiful girl by the name of violet.

She has long violet colored hair and beautiful rose petaled colored lips, that he one day dreams to kiss.

As time progresses violet grew quite fond of the man who was once a boy.

Violet and the man fall madly in love and become a couple.
